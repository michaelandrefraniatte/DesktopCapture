DesktopCapture

Capture Your Desktop

An Optimized Recorder for Your Desktop

The program capture video of your desktop and display your webcam if you have one. You can also capture only webcam. Each time with audio of course, with your audio stereo mixer, or your microphone, enable and set to default. A 4% background border, around your webcam, can be set, with whatever your gif file, even with animation, if present and if your choose to record your desktop, instead of your webcam. The numpad key dot, ., is used to start capture, the numpad key 0 is used to stop it, the numpad numbers are used to replace webcam around the screen, the numpad key 5 display the webcam in full screen on height axis and in center on width axis, the key multiply, *, is used to hide webcam, and the key substract, -, is used to change opacity, half to full. So, be ready, enable now your numpad ! Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

You need to close the webcam window if you want to record your webcam, instead of your desktop. For full optimized program, you need a animated GIF optimized for the border of webcam, it can be achieved with the web site ezgif.com. The webcam overlay is also available in a dedicated program.
